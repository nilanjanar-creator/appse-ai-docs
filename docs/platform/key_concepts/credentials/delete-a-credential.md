# Delete a Credential

You can **delete a credential** if itâ€™s no longer needed or used in any workflow. This will remove the saved connection from your account permanently.

## How to delete a credential

To delete a credential in appse ai, follow these simple steps:

1. Open the **three-dot menu** next to the credential you want to remove.  

<img src="\img\platform\key-concepts\credentials\delete-credential\select-three-dots.png" alt="appse ai credential select three dots" width="700"/>

2. Choose the **Delete** option.  

<img src="\img\platform\key-concepts\credentials\delete-credential\delete-option.png" alt="appse ai credential delete option" width="700"/>

3. A confirmation pop-up will appear, asking whether you want to **delete the credential permanently** or **cancel** the action.  

<img src="\img\platform\key-concepts\credentials\delete-credential\delete-pop-up.png" alt="appse ai credential delete popup" width="700"/>

4. Select **Delete** to permanently remove the credential from your account.

> Note: Any workflows using the deleted credential will return an error when executed.
